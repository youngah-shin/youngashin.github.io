Biography

I am a researcher at the School of Cybersecurity, Korea University. My research lies at the intersection of applied cryptography, security engineering, blockchain, and privacy-preserving artificial intelligence.

My doctoral research develops practical and provably secure online/offline cryptographic protocols for mobile applications. It focuses on identity-based multi-proxy signatures for Internet-of-Drones environments and identity-based on/offline signcryption secure against memory corruption between phases.

I also have experience in graduate teaching, international research coordination, academic conference organization, and cybersecurity outreach for micro, small, and medium-sized enterprises.
