Young-A Shin

Researcher in Applied Cryptography, Security, and Privacy

I am a researcher at the School of Cybersecurity, Korea University. My research focuses on cryptographic approaches to enhancing trust and privacy in emerging technologies, particularly the Internet of Things, federated learning, and blockchain.

I design advanced cryptographic primitives and privacy-preserving protocols with provable security guarantees. My current interests include identity-based cryptography, online/offline cryptographic protocols, scalable and auditable blockchain payments, and privacy-preserving interactions among autonomous agents.
